Objectives:

Advantage Physical Therapy approached me with a very simple, typical 1.0 website. A simple outdated design style. Disorganized content and an unstructured and confusing user flow. A lack of SEO strategy left them behind by their competition and deprived them of traffic. DJ, the owner, was looking to rebuild the website with some basic needs in mind:

  • Improve overall aesthetic and user experience to make the site more engaging and to drive trust and credibility.
  • Provide vital relevant information and for website visitors and capture leads via email signup and appointment request forms.
  • Provide information about the staff, facility and therapy services. Expedite the patient registration process with downloadable insurance forms
  • Create an SEO strategy to gain new business and compete with the substantial local competition.
  • Build a CMS simple enough for them to update and maintain basic content.

Scope:

  • Competitor research
  • User research
  • UI Design
  • Web Development
  • Client content administration
  • SEO strategy

Design Process:

With some basic color guidelines from the client to initiate the design process, I began with a vibrant and welcoming palette. I used colors to not only enhance the entire space but also used it to create an environment that was comfortable and tranquil to emulate the patient experience on site.

The client helped to define the user personas, so that we could construct a layout that would complement their needs. The majority of their patients were split in 2 groups. Young athletic group age 16-48. Typically with sports-related injuries. And an older group 49-65 suffering form chronic pain or accident-related physical issues. The preferred device user ratio was slightly titled to desktop, which prompted a design initiative beginning from a wider viewport down.

The initial landing page drew visitors with robust graphics and engaging icons to highlight the facilities therapy process and services. Results:

Since the launch of the website, Advantage Physical Therapy has expanded to a second location. Search rankings have improved from second page to top 10. Online appointments have accounted for over 25% of all new patients. Website conversion rates have risen to nearly 5%, putting the website into the top percentile. WordPress has allowed the client to successfully streamline content management to their satisfaction. .
