Newsday Mobile App

The first mobile app from Newsday brought all the elements of the website to a native app download. Design elements included page fronts, story galleries, story pages. Media content such as photo/video galleries. All of the challenges of delivering the news in a mobile format.

Project Overview

Dowens of different interfaces were needed to be developed and designed for the mobile app, so we could include as much functionality as possible from the website. Templates included Landing pages, story lists, articles and weather pages. We also needed to streamline the subscription process for ease of use on mobile devices. The firsty step was to create wireframes for each section using Adobe Illustrator. As each detail was finalized. The final designs were then created with pixel-perfect detail with Adobe Photoshop.

UI/UX Design

I designed interactive pages such as the Traffic Map, and the Weather forecast section. I created several different UI's depending on the users subscription level.

Designing for different devices

A completetly different UI was needed for the iPad version of the app. The larger viewport allowed for a better experience in terms of viewing media and other sections such as Traffic and Weather.
