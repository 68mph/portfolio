HEALTH & MEDICINE

Advantage Physical Therapy

Physical Therapy located in New Jersey. With two locations, they provide hands on treatent and are a quickly eexpanding business with a growing list of patients.

Objectives:

The client wanted to bring the website up to date with a more visually engaging appearance. They also wanted the ability to edit and update copy. Overall, their intention was to create a web presence that reflects the care and quality of their service.

  • Improve overall aesthetic and user experience to make the site more engaging and to drive trust and credibility.
  • Provide vital relevant information and for website visitors and capture leads via email signup and appointment request forms.
  • Provide information about the staff, facility and therapy services. Expedite the patient registration process with downloadable insurance forms
  • Create an SEO strategy to gain new business and compete with local competition.
  • Build a CMS simple enough for them to update and maintain basic content.

Scope:

  • Competitor research
  • User research
  • UI Design
  • Web Development
  • SEO strategy

Information Architecture:

The main function of the website to provide basic information about the benefits of physical therapy, the services they offer. we also gave users the ability to create appointments as well as the option to download the necessary forms needed to begin treatment.

  • Provide information about Physical therapy.
  • List of servcies provided by Advantage Physical Therapy.
  • Display images of the facility.
  • Direct users to the appropriate forms to schedule an appointment or seek advice.
  • Display recent patient testimonials.

Through careful research of the clients patient history, we established two main sets of potential website users.

  • Patients 45 years old and up, seeking to restore and improve functionality.
  • Younger adults in need of rehab for active and sports related injuries.

Design Process:

To create the color pallete. I established that a deep blue color would be the perfect primary. The color blue suggests Security, Trust, Stability, Loyalty, Wisdom, Confidence. All of the values that the client strives to achieve.

The client helped to define the user personas, so that we could construct a layout that would complement their needs. The majority of their patients were split in 2 groups. Young athletic group age 16-48. Typically with sports-related injuries. And an older group 49-65 suffering form chronic pain or accident-related physical issues. The preferred device user ratio was slightly titled to desktop, which prompted a design initiative beginning from a wider viewport down. The mobile version would be designed to deliver critical information quickly and effectively for each location.

The initial landing page drew visitors with robust graphics and engaging icons to highlight the facilities therapy process and services. Overview:

Once commissioned for this project, I carefully and thoroughly investigated their industry and local competition. I quickly established a plan to accomplish the online success they were hoping to achieve. Through survey and client feedback, I established the majority of their patients preferred to access their website by desktop computer. Using their current branding, I prototyped a desktop-first interface that presented their services and content in an engaging, intuitive experience. The client required a CMS to administrate content in-house and we established that WordPress was the best option for them.

Results:

Since the launch of the website, Advantage Physical Therapy has expanded to a second location. Search rankings have improved from second page to top 10. Online appointments have accounted for over 25% of all new patients. The WordPress CMS has been an effective tool for allowing the client to easily and successfully streamline content management to their satisfaction.
