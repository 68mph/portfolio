Brookhaven Opticians

Established almost a half century ago by Arsenio R. Mastrorocco in 1956, Brookhaven Opticians' mission statement and code of ethics is to provide quality precision eyewear with a concentration in filling difficult prescription eyewear, infant, and pediatric eyewear.

Objectives:

With four locations, Brookhaven Opticians are one of the biggest full-service eye care centers on Long Island, NY. With an outdated website, they sorely needed to update their online presence with a new website to reflect the quality of their service.

  • Improve overall aesthetic and user experience to make the site more engaging.
  • Create a better user experience to increase conversions.
  • Provide vital, relevant information for users to set up appointments for eye exams.
  • Drive traffic to the online shop for customers to browse and purchase eyewear directly from their website.
  • Build a CMS simple enough for them to update and maintain basic content.

Scope:

  • Competitor research
  • User research
  • UI Design
  • Web Development
  • Client content administration
  • SEO strategy

Critical issues with original website:

Not only was the old site badly outdated in terms of design. Content was outdated and poorly structured. Lack of an SEO strategy left the website with no visibility in ranking.

  • Navigation is confusing and poorly structured.
  • Branding is overly dominant and takes up too much space.
  • Home page has no useful content or CTA's.
  • Sign ups and scheduling too difficult to locate.
  • Footer is cluttered and difficult to read.
  • Interior content is sparely populated and outdated.
  • Poor SEO with a lack of relevant keywords.
  • Poor user experience inhibited users form purchasing eyewear from their online store.

Research:

Using Miro, we created a white board to visualize and plan out the website development. All relevant information is gathered and placed in a clear, visual presentation. Information about the client, company, current website are present. Style guides, stock imagery and mood boards are all visible for clear interpretation. Clients, designers and copywriters can communicate and interact through the entire design and development process.

Information Architecture:

The client had three very clear priorities to provide:

  • Provide eCommerce for users looking to purchase glasses online.
  • Help promote and schedule appointments for eye exams.
  • Provide full-service eye care for their kids brand "KIDS SPECS".

By analyzing the client's extensive customer history, we established three sets of user personas.

  • Seniors looking for eye care and purchase glasses.
  • Young adults interested in the latest brands and styles of glasses.
  • Children in need of full service eye care.

Style Guide:

The initial landing page drew visitors with robust graphics and CTA’s, to direct the user to relevant information points, services and inquiry forms and establish contact with the doctors and set appointments to expedite treatment.

Results:

Since the launch of the website, Advantage Physical Therapy has expanded to a second location. Search rankings have improved from second page to top 10. Online appointments have accounted for over 25% of all new patients. Website conversion rates have risen to nearly 5%, putting the website into the top percentile. WordPress has allowed the client to successfully streamline content management to their satisfaction. .
