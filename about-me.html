A little about me

For me, Interactive Design is about understanding people's needs.

Interactive design is all about understanding people's needs.

I am a web designer, graphic artist, UI/UX designer. I was born and raised on Long Island NY and I am currently living in Laguna Niguel CA. I work as a designer and WordPress developer for Electric Bricks, a NY-based media agency. I have a knack for designing to solve problems and creating intuitive, robust interfaces. It starts with listening to what the client needs and exploring solutions to the issue. I have designed for industries from Physical Therapy, Law Firms, Wineries, to large scale construction companies and news publishers.

Previously, I’ve had the opportunity to work as a Senior Designer at Newsday LLC, as well as several other agencies from small boutique sized companies to large firms.
