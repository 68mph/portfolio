Newsday App

The first mobile app from Newsday brought all the elements of the website to a native app download. Design elements included page fronts, story galleries, story pages. Media content such as photo/video galleries.

Objectives:

As a Lead Designer, I was tased with my team of 3 designers to recreate the same experience users have enjoyed with the successful Newsday website. Numerous interfaces and templates would have to be designed and developed for the mobile experience. Templates included landing pages, story lists, news story articles, weather pages and streaming traffic cameras. We also needed to streamline the subscription process for ease of use on mobile devices.

  • Improve overall aesthetic and user experience to make the site more engaging and to drive trust and credibility.
  • Provide vital relevant information and for website visitors and capture leads via email signup and appointment request forms.
  • Provide information about the staff, facility and therapy services. Expedite the patient registration process with downloadable insurance forms
  • Create an SEO strategy to gain new business and compete with the substantial local competition.
  • Build a CMS simple enough for them to update and maintain basic content.

Overview:

Offline reading

When in airplane mode or not connected to the Internet, all articles loaded when you were connected last will be available to read. Save any article, video or photo for viewing later.

Manage My Account

Update information and personalize subscription. Schedule a vacation hold on your print subscription, resolve a delivery issue, view and pay your bill, enroll in eBilling or EZ-Pay, update billing address and more.

Interactive weather map

Weather map can be easily customized to show you a variety of weather conditions on Long Island and around the world. Take a look at the future cast, which shows you what the next six hours of weather could bring and lets you easily track rain, snow and storms. You can save up to 10 locations for quick reference.

Sports scores

You can view full stats from all of your favorite pro sports teams on the scores pages and see all upcoming games.Manage your newsletters.

The Digital Paper

Open articles in easy-to-read story windows, download the paper to read offline and bookmark content to read later.

Results:

The app is available in the app store. Newsday subscribers have unlimited access to the app. Or, you can subscribe via iTunes for an all-access digital subscription. The app has received a solid 4.4 rating out of 7.5K Ratings and is one of the sot downloaded apps in the NY market.
