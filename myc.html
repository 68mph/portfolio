Moriches Yacht Club

Established over a century ago, Moriches Yacht Club has a rich tradition dedicated to yachting, sportsmanship, sociability, and recreation for its members and their children.

Objectives:

Moriches Yact Club came to us looking to redesign their current website with the following goals in mind:

  • Improve overall site architecture and navigation to enable easier browsing and a smoother experience.
  • Provide users with an easy path to apply for membership.
  • Provide information about the history, facility and services.
  • Provide an online shop for customers to browse and purchase branded products.
  • Implement a fully responsive solution backed by WordPress CMS.

Scope:

  • Competitor research
  • User research
  • Information architecture
  • UI design
  • WordPress theme development
  • SEO strategy

Critical issues with original website:

Not only was the old site badly outdated in terms of design. Content was outdated and poorly structured. Lack of an SEO strategy left the wesbsite with no visibility in ranking.

  • Navigation is confusing and over-conmplicated.
  • Home page has no useful content or CTA's.
  • Club membership application and memebrs services are diffucult to locate.
  • Interior content is sparely populated and outdated.
  • Graphic elements are redundant.
  • Lack of CTA's prohibited users from purchasing items from their online store.

Information Architecture:

The client had three very clear servicess to provide through the website:

  • Use strict brand guidelines of color palette and fonts throughout website.
  • Create CTA's to drive traffic to membership application.
  • Add calendar to provide up to date event information.
  • Promote and provide information about the club swimming pool.
  • Drive traffic to online store.
  • Leverage the WordPress CMS to enable clients to add and update website information.

Hi-fi Prototypes:

Located on the Great South Bay of Long Island, NY, it was important to represent the allure of the area with robust pictues of the bay, boating and the features of the yacht club. Light, crisp blues and aqua colors captures the essence of water life and the sailing community. Curves and fluid shapes were used to recreate the gently feel of sailing.

  • Introduced a hero slider element to provide relevent information abotu the club and membership options.
  • Display widgets to promote three top priorities: Club information, pool information and online store.
  • Billboard with CTA's to membership application.
  • Add widgets with recent events to events calendar page.
  • Quick form to enable customers to sign up for club newsletter.

Overview:

The new Moriches Yacht Club website feels fresh and fun, and captures the essence of the club's services and location. We were able to use WordPress to create and easyily managed events section to add information, photos and CTA's to make the interaction more engaging for website visitors. The content strategy is significantly stronger, with improved lateral navigation opportunities between information, events and application forms for new members.
